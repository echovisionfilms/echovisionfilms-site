Logline
A tense encounter between ambition and vulnerability unravels into a chilling power play, where control becomes the highest currency.

Synopsis
As Raj navigates the dynamics of his office, a quiet tension simmers beneath the surface. Carrot unfolds with precision, gradually revealing the cracks between appearance and reality. This suspenseful short film explores how power, ambition, and control manifest in unexpected ways.
