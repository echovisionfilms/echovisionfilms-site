About

Abhinav Gopisetty, the founder of Echo Vision Films, is a South Asian actor known for his work in the film Carrot (2025) and the television series Matlock (2024). Born and raised in Hyderabad, India, and now based in Los Angeles, Abhinav blends charm, emotional depth, and humor into thoughtful performances. Learn more on IMDb.

Echo Vision Films produces bold, provocative cinema that explores the intersection of power, ambition, and vulnerability. Our mission is to amplify voices and stories that challenge convention and linger long after the credits roll.

Beyond our flagship projects, Echo Vision Films provides end-to-end production services —including script development, casting, production management, cinematography, and post‑production — for narrative films, television and commercial content. We partner with visionary artists and align our work with values of authenticity, innovation and cinematic excellence.
